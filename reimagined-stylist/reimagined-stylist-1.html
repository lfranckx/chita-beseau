Created by Las Vegas-based creative colorist, influencer, and educator Chita Beseau, The Reimagined Stylist was vicariously founded through the lens of ambitious stylists seeking a higher vocational experience that goes beyond the basics and offers hands-on inspiration, career navigation and pragmatic solutions for reimagined career possibilities.

"I want to lead others from my own experience and teach them what I've learned along the way – that is what this program is all about."

–Chita Beseau

The Location

Salon Haus

Las Vegas, Nevada

The Reimagined Stylist will be hosted at Chita’s newly renovated salon and training academy – fully equipped with all the resources needed to create an immersive, hands-on experience.

Founder, Salon Owner, and Creative Colorist Chita Beseau is a believer by experience that continued investment in education elevates both the individual and collective success of her team and business. It's with this in mind that inspired the blueprint for her salon renovation, which includes a dedicated space for education.

The intimate space can comfortably accommodate up to twenty people for classroom-style learning. With direct access to the salon's amenities, it is the perfect location for students to experience the program's curriculum.

Choose Your Path

Hair Color, Vivids & Color Correction

Become THAT sought-after colorist and learn how to fearlessly create the most amazing color transformations, sure to bring new clients to your chair. Learn Chita’s best color practices, techniques and formulations for time efficiency, consultations, and the most demanded high-return color services – all while earning your client’s trust and loyalty.

Salon Business

Build your confidence and broaden your awareness as to what's happening in the industry today, what skills are going to bring you the most business, and how to set yourself up for success to reach your financial goals and dreams.

Social Media Branding

Learn how to leverage social media to start influencing NOW! It begins with marketing to your niche and owning and becoming a leader in that space. Let Chita teach you how to keep up the evolution of social media, stay within the algorithm, and how to take and post photographs of your work specifically for social media posting.

Styling & Retail

Take the foundations of blow drying for editorial and event hair and learn how to upgrade your blow drys in half the time. Also understand how you can incorporate easy, holistic shampooing rituals at your sink to create an unforgettable client experience, build client retention, and raise your prices.

Extensions

Chita takes all her years of perfecting her extension work and delivers it as a 100% hands-on workshop. Learn four extension techniques, debunk the myths of who is the potential hair extension client, and make extra profit by offering extensions as an add-on.
